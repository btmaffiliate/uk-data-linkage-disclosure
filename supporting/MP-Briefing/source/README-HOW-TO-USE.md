# MP Briefing Pack — Splink / probation-to-police pilot
### What's in this folder, and how to use it

**Context:** Noah Wild-Hebbs (Parliamentary Assistant to Iqbal Mohamed MP) asked for the briefing, FOI requests, and supporting information on the MoJ Splink "probation-to-police" pilot, and said the office can draft questions to the MoJ and look at raising it in Parliament. This pack is the response.

| File | What it is | How to use it |

| **00-COVER-EMAIL-to-Noah-Wild-Hebbs.md** | The reply email text | Paste into your email to Noah. Attach the PDF below. |
| **Parliamentary-Briefing-Pack-Splink.pdf** | The full pack as one clean, A4, print-ready document (13 pp) | **Attach this to the email.** It contains the briefing note, the draft WPQs, the FOI requests, and the evidence annex, formatted for a parliamentary reader. |

### The one thing to keep straight
**Splink = Ministry of Justice** (the probation-to-police pilot). **Palantir = Home Office / DHSC** (the trial the office already asked about). They are *separate systems with no documented link* — questions to the right department, so neither can deflect by answering about the other.

### Suggested order of play
1. Send the cover email + PDF to Noah.
2. He tables the **A block** (North Essex pilot) and **D block** (the research-vs-operational contradiction) WPQs first — the most concrete and hardest to dodge.
3. File the MoJ FOI in parallel.
4. Follow up on the WPQ answers — the value is often in what the Department *won't* confirm.
