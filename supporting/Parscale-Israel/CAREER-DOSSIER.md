# Brad Parscale — The Complete Information-Operations Record

*An exhaustive, public-records OSINT dossier on Brad Parscale's career in political data, advertising, and influence operations (2011–2026). Compiled June 2026 from nine parallel research streams, each adversarially verified.*

**Method & integrity.** Every claim is tiered — **[confirmed]** (primary document / multiple reputable outlets), **[reported]** (single credible outlet), **[alleged]** (claimed, contested, or unproven) — and every tactic is classified into one of four buckets, because conflating them is how this kind of work gets discredited:

- **DISINFORMATION** — demonstrably false content, deliberately spread.
- **MANIPULATIVE-BUT-LEGAL** — deceptive design/targeting that isn't false (dark posts, dark patterns, fake-persona outreach, AI-seeding).
- **AGGRESSIVE-LEGITIMATE** — hardball but truthful (industrial A/B testing, microtargeting on lawful data).
- **MYTHOLOGY** — widely claimed, NOT supported by the record.

Built from public sources only; allegations are labelled, not asserted. Not legal advice.

## Executive synthesis — the honest headline

Across a 20-year career, **Parscale's documented record is overwhelmingly *manipulative-but-legal political technology*, not a trail of fabricated "disinformation."** He is a *builder of targeting and persuasion machinery* — sold to whoever pays (the GOP, Argentina's Milei, Bolivia's Paz, Honduras's Asfura, the State of Israel). The machinery has grown steadily more deceptive at the edges — from 2016 dark posts, to dark-pattern-adjacent fundraising, to 2020 doctored attack ads, to 2025–26 fake-persona texting and AI-output seeding.

But the strict-sense **disinformation** in his orbit largely belongs to *others*: the Russian IRA (separate, no coordination — Mueller), the WinRed dark patterns (built by Coby/Lansing, not him), the AMMC "$617M shell" (Kushner-controlled), and the Brazil "stolen election" hoax (his business partner Fernando Cerimedo's act, not his). The genuinely **documented** false/manipulated content *attributable to his own operation* is narrower and specific (2020 doctored Biden ads; census-impersonation ads Facebook pulled), and the genuinely **novel and current** item is the **FARA-registered Israeli AI-influence apparatus** — disclosed, self-dealing, built to bend AI, of unproven effect.

The strongest, most defensible characterization is therefore: **a foreign-and-domestic political-influence contractor whose persuasion machine is increasingly deceptive by design, who now works for a foreign government, and who keeps company with documented disinformation operators — but whose own provable "disinformation" acts are fewer than the legend claims.** That is harder to dismiss precisely because it doesn't overreach.

## 1. 2016 — Project Alamo

- **[confirmed][AGGRESSIVE-LEGITIMATE]** San Antonio digital hub (Giles-Parscale, ~100 staff), ~$94M through the firm; Facebook ~80% of digital budget; raised $250M+ small-dollar. Industrial A/B testing: 50,000–60,000 ad variants on an average day, ~175,000 on the third-debate day (RNC's Gary Coby). *(CBS 60 Minutes; Wikipedia/Project Alamo.)*
- **[confirmed][MANIPULATIVE-BUT-LEGAL]** "Dark posts" — targeted ads visible only to the recipient, hiding messaging from public scrutiny. Legal then; the opacity is the concern (Facebook later required public ad archives).
- **[confirmed (existence) / reported (intent)][MANIPULATIVE-BUT-LEGAL]** Voter "deterrence": a senior official told Bloomberg "we have three major voter suppression operations under way" (idealistic white liberals, young women, African Americans). Channel 4's leaked dataset (2020) found ~3.5M Black Americans tagged **"Deterrence,"** over-represented vs. population. **But the content used was true-but-weaponized** (the "superpredator" ad used Clinton's real 1996 words) — manipulative targeting, **not fabricated disinformation.** The *causal* claim that it lowered Black turnout is **[alleged]**, unproven (multi-causal).
- **[confirmed]** No demonstrably-false fabricated content is attributable specifically to Parscale's 2016 operation.

## 2. Cambridge Analytica — de-mythologized

- **[confirmed]** CA was a paid vendor: **$5,912,500** total (FEC: the $5M payment was 1 Sept 2016), ~a dozen staff embedded under Matt Oczkowski.
- **[confirmed][MYTHOLOGY]** "Psychographics won 2016." Overstated and effectively debunked — Parscale, Oczkowski, *and Nix himself* later said psychographics weren't deployed in the general; the campaign switched to RNC data; Kogan called the method "a dead end"; the UK ICO found CA "dramatically overstated" its capabilities.
- **[confirmed][MYTHOLOGY]** "Trump targeting ran on the 87M harvested Facebook profiles." The harvest was real (Kogan/GSR) — but it was **CA's** data wrongdoing, and the **ICO, holding the seized servers, could not determine the data was used in any specific campaign.** Parscale's ads used Facebook's standard lawful Custom/Lookalike Audiences on RNC data. *Do not fuse the scandal (illicit harvest) with the campaign (lawful ad tools).*
