# city-of-london-investigation

Evidence-first, **non-accusatory** documentation of the **City of London Corporation**, on two tracks:

- **T1 — Data-linkage / data-sharing footprint:** the Corporation as a data controller, principally
  via the **City of London Police** (national lead force for fraud), the **National Fraud Intelligence
  Bureau (NFIB)**, and **Action Fraud → Report Fraud**.
- **T2 — Governance & transparency:** the Corporation's unique constitutional setup — **City's Cash**,
  the **business-vote franchise**, the **Remembrancer**, and **Freedom of Information** coverage.

We assemble sourced facts, grade confidence honestly, and turn gaps into FOI requests.
**We do not allege wrongdoing.** Where the record is silent, the answer is UNKNOWN, not an inference.

## Status taxonomy
| Status | Meaning | Bar |
|--------|---------|-----|
| CONFIRMED | Established fact | Primary/official source, live, content read |
| LIKELY | Strong inference / good secondary, or live-but-unread official page | — |
| UNKNOWN | No sourced answer → FOI | Never guessed |
| SPECULATION | Explicit, labelled hypothesis | Never as fact |

## Method note
Several official pages (`reportfraud.police.uk`, `cityoflondon.gov.uk`) block the fetch client used
here but resolve fine in a browser (verified HTTP 200 by the checker). Claims resting only on those
pages are graded **LIKELY** (live URL, full text not yet read), not CONFIRMED. Three sources that
blocked *every* client are logged in `raw_links.json` `_meta.unverified_dropped`, not cited.
