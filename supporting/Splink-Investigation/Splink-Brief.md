# The Consent Gap
### Governments are linking their citizens' records into single profiles — and nobody was asked.

**One tool, one window into a global default.** *Splink* — a free record-linkage program built by the
UK Ministry of Justice — joins a person's separate records across government where there is no shared ID.
Everything below is from the institutions' own published documents.

---

**1. They linked 56 million people — silently.**
The UK Office for National Statistics linked the **2021 Census to DWP benefits and HMRC tax records** —
**96.7%, ~56 million people.** No individual consent. No notification. No opt-out.
*(ONS, 2021 Census↔DWP/NINo methodology)*

**2. It's no longer just statistics — it's identifying named individuals.**
The Ministry of Justice uses it **in real time in court**, and "to find **Police National Computer
numbers** associated with individuals, in order to request relevant arrest information from the police."
*(GOV.UK Algorithmic Transparency Record — MoJ Splink Master Record)*

**3. It now reaches the most vulnerable people alive.**
Documented users include **UNHCR (refugee data)**, the **Shared Child Health Record in Laos**, and the
**2024 Gambian census.** *(Splink official Use Cases)*

**4. It's not one country.**
Confirmed government use across **~8 countries plus the EU and the UN** — UK, Australia, **Germany
(Destatis)**, USA (Defense Health Agency, 200M+ records), **Canada** (Ontario; Environment Canada),
Chile, Gambia, Laos, the **European Medicines Agency**, **UNHCR.** And that is only who *published* it.
*(Splink official Use Cases)*

**5. The bigger truth: this is the new normal.**
**~54% of UNECE countries already build their census on linked administrative data; ~half go *fully*
register-based by 2030.** The Nordics have for decades. OECD and the UN endorse it "subject to
safeguards." *(UNECE 2020 census round; OECD/UN guidance)*

**6. The legal basis is the scandal.**
None of this is illegal. It runs on **statute, not consent.** Which means the consent gap isn't a few
bad actors — it's **structural**, the default setting of the modern state, expanding toward total
coverage.

---

### The line that doesn't move:
**Your records — health, tax, justice, benefits — are being joined into a single profile of you, lawfully,
across most of the developed world. You were never asked. You cannot say no. And almost no one was told.**
